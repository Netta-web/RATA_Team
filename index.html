OUR TEAM

Meet the People Driving Change

Our dedicated team of professionals works tirelessly to ensure that every Rwandan with a disability has access to the assistive technology they need to thrive, participate, and lead fulfilling lives.

Empowering Through Technology

Rwanda Assistive Technology Association (RATA) is a leading non-profit organization dedicated to improving the lives of people with disabilities through access to appropriate, affordable, and sustainable assistive technology solutions.

Since our founding, we have served thousands individuals across multiple districts of Rwanda, providing mobility aids, communication devices, educational tools, and training that promote independence and inclusion.

Our work is guided by the principles of user-centered design, community participation, and evidence-based advocacy. We believe that access to assistive technology is a fundamental human right that enables full participation in society.
